> And at the **top** — the emotions and associations: freedom, nostalgia, a warm summer evening.

🔑 *«Just as a perfumer breaks down a scent into notes, we break down the taste of beer into layers — from a simple sensation to an emotion.»*

*(Клик → Слайд 4)*

## 💬 Q&A — Готовые ответы на вопросы жюри

> [!TIP]
> Формула ответа: **Подтверди → Ответь → Развей.**
> Пример: *«Great question. The answer is… And beyond that…»*

### 1. «Чем отличаетесь от Untappd / RateBeer?»

> Untappd is a social network — users check in and rate beers 1-5. We're a **sensory education platform**. We don't ask «did you like it?» — we ask «what did you feel?» That's a fundamentally different product category. Also, RateBeer shut down in 2025 — the space is wide open.

### 2. «Как привлечёте пользователей?»

> Three channels. **First** — through Efes itself: 15 brands, millions of consumers, existing marketing channels. **Second** — the Sommelier School creates organic retention through gamification. **Third** — Flavor DNA is designed for viral sharing — like Spotify Wrapped, people want to show their taste identity.

### 3. «Можно ли это масштабировать?»

> Absolutely. The flavor pyramid model is **category-agnostic**. Phase 1 is beer. Phase 2 — coffee and wine. Phase 3 — Turkey, Uzbekistan, the entire Anadolu footprint. The education framework and recommendation engine work for any beverage.

### 4. «Как вы собираете данные о вкусовых профилях?»

> Three sources. **Expert data** — certified tasting notes from brewmasters and sommeliers. **Community data** — user tasting responses (what do YOU feel?). **Scientific data** — flavor wheel taxonomies and ingredient analysis. Over time, the AI refines profiles based on thousands of user inputs.

### 5. «Кто ваш главный конкурент?»

> Honestly? **Ignorance.** There is no platform doing sensory education for beer. Untappd does ratings. Vivino does it for wine but focuses on price, not education. We're creating a **new category** — and the first mover in the CIS region.

### 6. «Что насчёт ответственного потребления?»

> This is actually core to our mission. Flavor Tree teaches people to drink **less, but better**. When you understand taste, you value quality over quantity. Conscious consumption IS responsible consumption. We're aligned with global trends and Efes's CSR goals.

### 7. «Почему вы, а не кто-то с опытом в FoodTech?»

> Because this idea was born from passion, not from a corporate lab. We understand the **consumer perspective** — we ARE the consumers.
